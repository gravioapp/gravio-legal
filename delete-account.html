Step-by-step instructions (in-app interface labels remain in Russian)

Note: the internal app buttons and section names are shown in Russian exactly as they appear in the application.

  1. Open the Gravio app.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Go to Профиль (Profile).
  4. Tap О приложении (About App).
  5. Select Отозвать согласие и удалить данные (Withdraw consent and delete data).
  6. Confirm deletion in the dialog that appears.

If you cannot delete via the app

If you cannot perform deletion in the app (for example, you have lost access to the account or there is a technical issue), send an email with the subject "Account deletion request — Gravio" to: gravio.app@gmail.com. In the message, please include the email address associated with the account and a brief explanation of the problem.

Data that WILL be deleted

  • Account and profile information (name, email, identifiers).
  • Uploaded documents (passport scans, medical certificates, authorizations, etc.).
  • Event applications and attached files.
  • Personal data related to event participation (contacts, vehicle information, etc.).
  • Personal data stored in active profiles and service records.

Data that MAY remain or be anonymized

  • Competition results and public statistics will remain as public information but will be anonymized — the name will be replaced with "Deleted User" or "Deleted Participant".
  • Certain technical information (logs, backups) may be retained for a limited time in anonymized form for security and recovery purposes.
  • Data required to be retained by law (for example, accounting records) will be kept to the extent necessary to comply with legal obligations.

Request processing time

We process account deletion requests within 30 calendar days from the moment the request is confirmed. Some data (backups, logs, data required to be retained by law) may be kept longer within the limits set by applicable law.

Verification and confirmations

To prevent unauthorized deletions we may request identity confirmation or additional information (for example, profile photo, recent activity, or the account email) before proceeding with deletion. These verification items are used solely to confirm ownership and are not retained longer than necessary to process the request.
