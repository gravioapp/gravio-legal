11. Data Retention Periods

We store your personal data for the following periods:

Data Type Retention Period
Account data (profile, email) Until account deletion by user OR 6 months from last activity
User documents 6 months from last event participation
Chat messages 6 months from sending OR deleted with event chat
Event chats Deleted 7 days after event completion
Application documents Deleted 7 days after event completion
Support correspondence 1 year from last contact
Logs and technical data 6 months
Tournament results Indefinitely (public information, anonymized after account deletion)

Despite the measures taken, absolute security on the Internet does not exist. We recommend using strong passwords and not sharing credentials with third parties.

16. Managing Advertising Settings

You can manage ad personalization in your device settings. The Application will automatically take your choice into account.

How to disable personalized advertising:

On iPhone/iPad (iOS):

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Go to Privacy & Security
  3. Select Apple Advertising
  4. Enable Limit Ad Tracking

On Android:

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Go to Google
  3. Select Ads
  4. Enable Opt out of Ads Personalization

What happens after disabling:

  • Ads will continue to be shown, but without considering your interests
  • Yandex SDK automatically sees your choice and shows non-personalized ads
  • You do not need to additionally configure anything in the Gravio app

Important: These settings are located on your device, not in the app. We do not add a separate button to disable ads, as this is already implemented at the system level.
